Crowd Movement Simulation at Railway Infrastructures

  • Formulated an Agent-Based simulation model to explain different phenomena in an evacuation scenario on Railway platforms
  • Used multi-agent programmable language and modeling environment Netlogo
  • Different parameter were observed through simultion such as impatience level of commuters in congested situation and injured person ratio for evacuation situation etc.
  • Extracted data of pedestrians speed, flow, and density for the new and old Delhi railway station to obtain fundamental relationships between these different parameters and these observed data helped to create simulation
